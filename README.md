# 🌍 Geospatial Data Analysis of Restaurants – Zomato Dataset

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs **geospatial data analysis on restaurant data from Zomato** to understand restaurant distribution, cuisine popularity, and location-based trends across cities. The goal is to use **geographical and business data** to uncover patterns in restaurant availability, customer preferences, and food trends.

Using **Python and geospatial visualization techniques**, the project explores how restaurant characteristics vary across locations and how geography influences dining patterns.

---

## 📊 Data Source

The dataset contains restaurant information from **Zomato**, including:

### Key Attributes

* `Restaurant Name` – Name of the restaurant
* `City` – City where the restaurant is located
* `Locality` – Area within the city
* `Latitude` – Geographic latitude coordinate
* `Longitude` – Geographic longitude coordinate
* `Cuisines` – Types of cuisines offered
* `Average Cost for Two` – Price indicator
* `Aggregate Rating` – Customer rating score
* `Votes` – Number of customer votes
* `Has Online Delivery` – Online delivery availability
* `Has Table Booking` – Reservation availability

These attributes allow analysis of **restaurant density, cuisine popularity, and geographic distribution**.

## 📈 Key Insights

* Restaurants tend to cluster in **urban commercial areas with higher demand**.
* Certain cuisines dominate specific regions, reflecting **local food preferences**.
* Highly rated restaurants often receive significantly higher vote counts.
* Online delivery services are more common in **high-density restaurant areas**.
* Pricing varies significantly depending on location and restaurant type.
